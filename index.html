About Me

Life-long Texan here. Born and raised in Dallas, I moved in 1999 to attend UT Austin. Though I graduated in 2003 with a degree in architectural engineering, I followed the entrepreneurial route, doing finance, admin, and bookkeeping in addition to programming work for my co-owned businesses. My attention to detail and desire to genuinely help people, along with my love of numbers, led me to be more on the admin side of the businesses - finances, bookkeeping, including updating websites and programming CRMs. Anyone with a small business understands the need to wear many hats!

In recent years I happily shifted my focus to more programming and much less bookkeeping. My passion for solving problems while being creative is a great fit for designing and programming websites. Taking classes at Austin Coding Academy has been a great way to increase my knowledge and get to know the industry better. I get to play all day doing something I love and earn a living. How many people can say that?

I live in SW Austin with my fiance, two daughters, and two stepdaughters. When not working, I enjoy reading, cooking, spending time with my family, and I'm currently training for my first 5K. My personal life is filled with fun, love, soccer practice and Girl Scouts, and I wouldn't have it any other way.
